What is Chance?

Chance is a social media platform where you can share your photos.

As a social media, Chance includes:

  • Profiles
  • Friends
  • Notifications
  • Algorithm
  • Discover
  • Chat
  • Followers, following, comments, likes , favorites, views, downloads and posts

    • With Chance's unique algorithm, it's easy to enhance your profile's popularity effortlessly.

    First steps to something great

  • If you are logged in, you will not be able to see the login page
    1. Open the login page
    2. Write down your information

    The username must be 3 to 20 characters long and contain no spaces.
    Password must be 8+ characters with at least 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, and 1 special character.
    The email address must be in a valid format and have a domain from either 'gmail.com' or 'yahoo.com'.
    User must be at least 13 years old.
